Overview

In this website, I highlight one of my main interests: my favorite band, The Doors. The site offers a short summary of their music career, lists some of their most impactful music and the members that formed their sound and their legacy.

Why This Project

I chose The Doors for this project because they have had a strong influence on my appreciation for music. Their distinctive sound and lyrical poetry made them stand out of the rest of the bands of their era. Doing this site enabled me not only to investigate their history, but also to use the concepts of HTML and CSS, in a purposeful and personal manner.

What You'll Find on This Site
  • This site is an overview of the musical journey and the impact of The Doors.
  • Visitors will be able to learn about the history of the band.
  • Get to know their most influential albums and also about the members that helped the group in shaping the characteristic voice of the band.
How to Explore This Site

This site is categorized into parts that showcase various elements of the legacy of The Doors. Every page is devoted to some important aspect of their story, and one can easily explore their music and background.
