Featured Songs and Albums

The Doors released six studio albums during their career, each of them with a unique sound and style. Some of their most influential songs include "Light My Fire," "Break on Through (To the Other Side)," "Riders on the Storm," "People Are Strange," and "The End." These songs are considered classics of rock music and have been covered by many artists over the years. The Doors' music continues to be celebrated for its innovation, poetic lyrics, and powerful performances.
